Craft breweries like Logboat worried that Missourians were going to have a harder time accessing their products at local grocery stores. That’s because legislation was pending that would extend the ability to allow beer companies to lease coolers, or refrigerator units, to stores.

The coolers are free advertising for whoever owns them because they’re typically covered with logos.
